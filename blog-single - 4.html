How to Stay Motivated to Workout Throughout Your Cycle

24 February 2025 Joan Cena Tag: Body Fitness, General Health

      It’s normal to feel unmotivated and drained on some days and feel unstoppable on others. Your menstruation cycle affects these fluctuations. Learn how to stay motivated by tuning into your body’s needs.

1. Set Realistic Goals for Each Phase

Your energy level shifts throughout your cycle, and it’s important to adjust your fitness goals accordingly.

  • Menstrual phase: Focus on consistency with gentle workouts and low-impact movements.
  • Follicular phase: Ideal time to achieve personal goals that require strength and endurance.
  • Ovulation phase: Maximize motivation; it could also be the perfect time to challenge yourself.
  • Luteal phase: Prioritize movement that feels good, such as stretching and light resistance training.
2. Find Workouts You Enjoy

During low-energy moments, doing something you love will help you feel more motivated.

  • Engaging activities: Try swimming, dancing, and outdoor activities for a fun workout experience.
  • Keep it exciting: Switch up your routine to prevent boredom.
  • Boost your mood: Listening to energizing music or motivating podcasts can enhance your workout.
  • Stay accountable: Exercising with a friend or group can help maintain motivation.
3. Build a Support System

Surrounding yourself with supportive people can provide encouragement and help you stay motivated.

  • Workout partners: Exercising with a friend can help you stay on track.
  • Digital inspiration: Follow fitness influencers who inspire and motivate you.
  • Join a fitness community: Being part of a group offers support, motivation, and the opportunity to share progress and tips.
